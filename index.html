Experimental Procedure

  1. Enter your name and select the set list number (choose any number you like).
  2. Press "Start Experiment" button.
  3. Listen to the music samples and rate the jazz elements.
  4. Press "next" button and start next evaluation.
  5. When you finish this experiment, press "finish" button and download the result.
    Send it to Minseok Kim on slack or the following email address.
  6. Email address: kim.minseok@g.sp.m.is.nagoya-u.ac.jp

Notes

  • This experiment takes no more than 10 minutes.
  • Please do not update the page during this experiment.
  • You may not be able to download the results on your phone, so please use your PC.
  • Please use headphones for this experiment.
